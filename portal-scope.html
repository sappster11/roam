Definition: "Net New" Campaign

A single email asset counts as two (2) distinct creative versions if it includes substantially different openings, restructured body copy, or a visually distinct layout.

Minor edits (such as subject-line swaps, tone tweaks, image replacements, or CTA text changes that do not alter structure or concept) count as one (1) version.

Practical Benchmark

If 30% or more of an email's content, structure, or messaging intent changes, it is deemed a new asset.
