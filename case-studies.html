01 // THE SIGNAL

The "Leaky Bucket" Paradox.

Velvet was scaling aggressively on paid social, acquiring 5,000+ new customers monthly. However, their 60-day retention rate was plummeting. They were filling the bucket with expensive ads, but the bottom had fallen out.

The Audit revealed: 70% of one-time buyers never received a personalized second-purchase offer tailored to their specific category entry point.

02 // THE ARCHITECTURE

Predictive Win-Back.

We didn't just send more emails. We engineered a logic layer based on purchase intent.

  • Intent Scoring We segmented users by LTV potential, not just purchase history. High-value prospects received VIP concierge SMS flows.
  • Cross-Channel Pacing We synchronized Email and SMS to ensure no collision. SMS was reserved for the "critical decision window" (days 21-30).
03 // THE REALITY

+$2.4M in Found Revenue.

Within 90 days, the new infrastructure generated an incremental $2.4M that would have otherwise been lost to churn. The system now runs on autopilot, continuously optimizing based on new customer cohorts.
